Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a tomato-based sauce with butter and cream.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • Tomato puree
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Kasuri methi
  • Garam masala
  • Chili powder
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions, ginger-garlic paste until golden brown.
  2. Add tomato puree, chili powder, garam masala, and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add cream and water to adjust the consistency. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add paneer cubes and cook for 10 more minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and kasuri methi. Serve hot with naan or rice.
