Description

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Bhature dough (flour, yogurt, baking soda)
  • Cilantro
  • Green chilies

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight, then boil them until soft.
  2. Prepare the bhature dough by kneading it and letting it rise.
  3. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© onions and ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add tomatoes, garam masala, and cook until the tomatoes soften.
  5. Mix the chickpeas with the spices and cook for 15 minutes.
  6. Fry the bhature in hot oil and serve with the chole and cilantro garnish.
