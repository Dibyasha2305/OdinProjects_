Butter Chicken

Home

Description

Butter chicken, also known as murgh makhani, is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Tomato puree
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Garam masala
  • Cilantro
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Onions
  • Cumin powder

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and salt for a few hours.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan with some oil until golden brown.
  3. In a separate pan, melt butter and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger.
  4. Add tomato puree, garam masala, cumin powder, and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add cream and cooked chicken, cook for another 15 minutes.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and kasuri methi. Serve with naan or rice.
