Biryani

Home

Description

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish with spices, meat (usually chicken or mutton), and a mixture of herbs and yogurt.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Garam masala

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt, spices, and mint for a few hours.
  2. Cook the rice with whole spices until 70% cooked.
  3. In a large pot, layer rice and marinated meat, cooking each layer.
  4. Cook the biryani on low heat for 20 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fried onions, mint, and coriander. Serve with raita.
